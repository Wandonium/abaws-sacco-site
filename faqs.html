Frequently Asked Questions

To become a member, you need to fill out the membership application form available on our downloads page, attach a copy of your ID, and pay the required membership fee. Our team will then process your application.

Members enjoy numerous benefits including access to affordable loans, earning annual dividends on shares and interest on deposits, opportunities for savings, and being part of a supportive community focused on financial growth.

You can apply for a loan by filling out the loan application form and submitting it to our offices. You must be a member and have met the minimum savings requirements for the specific loan product you are interested in.

Loan processing times vary depending on the type of loan. Emergency loans are typically processed within 24-48 hours, while development and business loans may take a few days to a week, subject to meeting all requirements.
