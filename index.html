Welcome to Mumias ABAWS Sacco

Mumias Abaws Sacco is a Savings and Credit Co-operative Society in Kenya, located in the town of Shianda, Lusheya/Lubinu ward, Mumias East Sub County, Kakamega County. Mumias Abaws Sacco was formed in 2022 and subsequently registered on 21st October 2022 under the Cooperative Society Act Cap 490 and obtained its registration (CS/No 27368) from the Commissioner of Cooperatives. The main objective of Mumias Abaws Sacco Society is to promote thrift among its members by affording them an opportunity for accumulating their savings and deposits and create thereby a source of funds from which loans can be made available to them for provident and productive purposes, at fair and reasonable rates of interest, thereby enabling them to use and control their money for their mutual benefit.
