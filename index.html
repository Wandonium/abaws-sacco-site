Welcome to Mumias Abaws Sacco

Established in 1972, our Sacco is a leading savings and credit cooperative society in Kenya. Our primary objective is to promote thrift among our members by affording them an opportunity for accumulating their savings and deposits and in return providing them with credit facilities for development and provident purposes.
